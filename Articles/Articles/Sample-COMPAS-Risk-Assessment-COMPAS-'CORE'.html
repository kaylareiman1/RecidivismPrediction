COMPAS "CORE" risk and needs assessment
Northpointe's core product is a 137-question "CORE" survey that asks for information ranging from a defendant's criminal history to his or her social life and thoughts. This version was obtained from Wisconsin, a state that uses COMPAS at every stage of the criminal justice system after conviction.
